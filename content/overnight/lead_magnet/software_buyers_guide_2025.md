## Chapter 1: Project Management  

### **Notion**  
- **Pricing**: Free (for basic use); $12/user/month (Business plan).  
- **Key Features**: All-in-one workspace for tasks, docs, databases, and team collaboration.  
- **Pros**: Customizable templates, real-time collaboration, integrates with 1,000+ apps.  
- **Cons**: Steeper learning curve for beginners.  

### **Trello**  
- **Pricing**: Free (basic); $12.50/user/month (Business Class).  
- **Key Features**: Visual boards, drag-and-drop interface, integrations with Slack and Google Workspace.  
- **Pros**: Intuitive for simple workflows; ideal for solo freelancers.  
- **Cons**: Limited advanced task management features.  

### **Asana**  
- **Pricing**: Free (for up to 15 users); $10.99/user/month (Premium).  
- **Key Features**: Task automation, timeline views, project tracking.  
- **Pros**: Scalable for growing teams; robust reporting.  
- **Cons**: Overwhelming for small teams with limited budgets.  

---

## Chapter 2: Design  

### **Canva**  
- **Pricing**: Free (basic tools); $15/month (Pro Plan).  
- **Key Features**: Drag-and-drop design, 100M+ templates, branding kits.  
- **Pros**: Ideal for social media, presentations, and non-designers.  
- **Cons**: Limited advanced editing tools.  

### **Adobe Express**  
- **Pricing**: Free (basic); $9.99/month (Adobe Express Pro).  
- **Key Features**: AI-powered tools, video editing, brand assets.  
- **Pros**: Adobe quality at a lower price; great for video creators.  
- **Cons**: Less intuitive than Canva for beginners.  

### **Figma**  
- **Pricing**: Free (for individuals); $12/user/month (Team plan).  
- **Key Features**: Real-time collaboration, prototyping, design systems.  
- **Pros**: Industry standard for UI/UX designers; seamless team workflows.  
- **Cons**: No free version for teams; limited to design-focused work.  

---

## Chapter 3: Communication  

### **Slack**  
- **Pricing**: Free (for up to 10 users); $12.50/user/month (Standard).  
- **Key Features**: Channels, file sharing, integrations with Trello and Notion.  
- **Pros**: Excellent for team communication; searchable history.  
- **Cons**: No native video calls (requires Zoom integration).  

### **Discord**  
- **Pricing**: Free (basic); $12/user/month (Nitro for teams).  
- **Key Features**: Voice and video calls, community servers, screen sharing.  
- **Pros**: Ideal for remote teams and communities; low-latency voice.  
- **Cons**: Less structured than Slack for work tasks.  

### **Microsoft Teams**  
- **Pricing**: Free (for small teams); $10/user/month (Microsoft 365).  
- **Key Features**: Deep integration with Office 365, video conferencing, app store.  
- **Pros**: Best for businesses already using Microsoft tools.  
- **Cons**: Overloaded with features for simple teams.  

---

## Chapter 4: File Storage  

### **Google Drive**  
- **Pricing**: Free (15 GB); $1.99/month/100 GB (Google One).  
- **Key Features**: Seamless G Suite integration, real-time docs, version history.  
- **Pros**: Best for Google Workspace users; strong collaboration.  
- **Cons**: Limited offline access without premium plans.  

### **Dropbox**  
- **Pricing**: Free (2 GB); $11.99/month (Business Starter).  
- **Key Features**: Smart sync, file recovery, team folders.  
- **Pros**: Reliable for file sharing; excellent customer support.  
- **Cons**: Higher prices for large teams.  

### **OneDrive**  
- **Pricing**: Free (5 GB); $6.99/month (1 TB, Microsoft 365).  
- **Key Features**: Tight Office 365 integration, automatic backups.  
- **Pros**: Best for Windows users; secure for sensitive files.  
- **Cons**: Slower performance on non-Microsoft platforms.  

---

## Chapter 5: Website Builders  

### **WordPress**  
- **Pricing**: Free (hosting required); $2.95/month (hosting + themes).  
- **Key Features**: Customizable with plugins, SEO-friendly, e-commerce capabilities.  
- **Pros**: Unlimited scalability; vast community support.  
- **Cons**: Requires technical know-how or hiring a developer.  

### **Webflow**  
- **Pricing**: Free (basic); $15/month (CMS plan).  
- **Key Features**: No-code design, responsive templates, SEO tools.  
- **Pros**: Ideal for designers; full control over code.  
- **Cons**: Expensive for basic websites.  

### **Squarespace**  
- **Pricing**: $12/month (basic plan).  
- **Key Features**: Beautiful templates, built-in blogging, e-commerce.  
- **Pros**: Stunning designs; beginner-friendly.  
- **Cons**: Limited customization compared to WordPress.
