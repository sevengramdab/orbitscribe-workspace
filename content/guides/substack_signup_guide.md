# Substack Account Setup & Publishing Guide

## Overview

Substack is a newsletter + blog platform that lets writers publish directly to subscribers via email and the web. It offers **free subscriptions** (growth) and **paid subscriptions** (direct revenue).

For our monetization swarm, Substack serves as:
- An owned audience (email list = highest-value asset)
- A direct revenue stream via paid subscriptions
- A distribution channel for affiliate content
- SEO-rich long-form hosting

---

## Step-by-Step Account Creation

### 1. Go to Substack.com
- Visit [substack.com](https://substack.com)
- Click **Start a publication**

### 2. Create Your Publication
Fill in:
- **Publication name**: See naming suggestions below
- **Byline**: Your name or pen name
- **Description**: See pre-filled template below

### 3. Set Up Your Profile
Click **Settings** → **Publication details**

**Logo/Icon**: Upload a square image (256x256px minimum)
**Cover image**: 1200x400px banner for your publication page
**About page**: See template below

### 4. Configure Email Settings
- **From name**: How your name appears in inboxes
- **Reply-to**: Where reader replies go
- **Email footer**: Add your disclosure here

### 5. Set Up Payments (Optional)
- Go to **Settings** → **Payments**
- Connect a **Stripe account**
- Set subscription price (recommend starting at **$5/month** or **$50/year**)
- Substack takes 10% + Stripe fees

### 6. Connect Custom Domain (Optional)
- Go to **Settings** → **Domain**
- Follow DNS instructions
- Cost: ~$12/year via Namecheap/Cloudflare

### 7. Verify Email
- Check inbox for Substack confirmation
- Click verification link

### 8. Done
Publish your first post.

## Publishing Best Practices

### Post Types That Work
| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Comparison posts** | Weekly | SEO traffic + affiliate revenue |
| **Newsletter updates** | Weekly | Engagement + subscriber growth |
| **Deals/roundups** | Bi-weekly | Urgency-driven clicks |
| **How-to guides** | Monthly | Authority + long-tail SEO |

### SEO on Substack
- Custom subdomains rank well on Google
- Use descriptive URLs (`/p/bluehost-vs-siteground` not `/p/post-123`)
- Add tags (Substack uses these as categories)
- Cross-link between posts

### Email Optimization
- **Subject lines**: 40-60 characters, specific benefit
- **Preview text**: First 100 characters matter most
- **Send time**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM EST
- **Images**: Keep under 1MB, use descriptive filenames

### Affiliate Strategy
- Substack **allows** affiliate links
- Must include disclosure (Substack requires this in their ToS)
- Best placement: mid-post contextual links + bottom CTA
- Track clicks with UTM parameters
