# Medium Account Setup & Publishing Guide

## Overview

Medium is a free publishing platform with 100M+ monthly readers. It offers the **Medium Partner Program** (MPP) which pays writers based on member reading time, plus the ability to promote affiliate products within stories.

For our monetization swarm, Medium serves as:
- A traffic source for affiliate content
- A direct revenue stream via MPP
- A backlink generator for SEO authority

---

## Step-by-Step Account Creation

### 1. Go to Medium.com
- Visit [medium.com](https://medium.com)
- Click **Get started** or **Sign up**

### 2. Choose Sign-Up Method
- **Recommended**: Sign up with **Google** (faster, no email verification delay)
- Alternative: Email + password

### 3. Complete Profile Setup
Click your avatar → **Settings** → **Profile**

**Name**: Use your real name or brand name
**Bio**: See pre-filled bio template below
**Avatar**: Upload a professional headshot or logo (400x400px)

### 4. Set Up the Partner Program (Optional but Recommended)
- Go to [medium.com/partner-program](https://medium.com/partner-program)
- Click **Apply**
- Connect a **Stripe account** for payouts
- You need:
  - 100+ followers (to unlock MPP)
  - At least 1 published story
  - Stripe-supported country

### 5. Verify Email
- Check your inbox for Medium verification
- Click the confirmation link

### 6. Done
You can now publish stories.

## Publishing Best Practices

### Story Formatting
- Use a **catchy headline** (60-80 characters)
- Add a **featured image** (1200x630px performs best)
- Break text into short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Use **H2/H3 subheadings** for scannability
- Add **alt text** to images for accessibility

### SEO on Medium
- Use 3-5 **tags** per story (Medium allows 5)
- Include keywords naturally in the first 100 words
- Cross-link to your other Medium stories
- Add a **canonical link** if republishing from your own blog

### Affiliate Link Rules on Medium
- Medium **allows** affiliate links in stories
- You **must** include a clear disclosure near the link
- Do not use link shorteners (Medium may flag them)
- Maximum 1-2 affiliate links per 500 words (best practice)

### Optimal Publishing Schedule
- **Best days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times**: 6–9 AM EST (US audience)
- **Frequency**: 2-3 stories per week minimum for growth
