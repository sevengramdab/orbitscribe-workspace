# ClickBank Affiliate Sign-Up & Integration Guide

## Overview

ClickBank is one of the largest and oldest digital product affiliate marketplaces, founded in 1998. It specializes in info-products (e-books, online courses, software, memberships) and offers some of the highest commission rates in the industry — commonly 50% to 75% per sale. It is available in 190+ countries and has paid out over $6 billion to affiliates and vendors.

For our content niches — software/tools, VPNs, web hosting, and design software — ClickBank offers complementary digital products such as training courses, design asset packs, cybersecurity guides, and website-building tools that can be promoted alongside physical product comparisons.

## Step-by-Step Account Creation

### 1. Initiate Sign-Up
- Go to [clickbank.com](https://www.clickbank.com) and click **Register** / **Sign Up**.
- You can sign up as an affiliate, vendor, or both. For our purposes, select **Affiliate**.

### 2. Enter Basic Information
Fill out the registration form with:
- Full legal name
- Country of residence
- Phone number
- Valid email address
- Password

### 3. Accept Terms and Conditions
- Review the ClickBank terms.
- Click **Join ClickBank!** to create your primary account.

### 4. Complete Your Client Profile
- Log in and navigate to the **Profile** tab.
- Enter your payee name and billing address.
- Indicate that you will use the account to **promote products as an affiliate**.
- Save your profile.

### 5. Create an Account Nickname
- Go to the **Accounts** tab and click **Create an Account**.
- Select **Affiliate** from the **Account Type** dropdown.
- Choose a unique account nickname (this will be used in your HopLinks).
- Click **Sign Up**.

> **Tip:** You only need one account nickname to promote multiple products. Creating too many nicknames can delay payments because you must reach the payment threshold and customer distribution requirement on each one.

### 6. Verify Your Email
- Confirm your email address in the same browser on a desktop before setting up payments.

### 7. Set Up Payment Information
- Click the **Accounts** tab and select your nickname.
- Go to **Account Settings** → **Payment Information** → **Edit**.
- Choose your preferred payout method and set your payment threshold.
- Save changes.

### 8. Done
You now have access to the ClickBank Marketplace and can start generating affiliate links.

## How to Find High-Gravity Products in Our Niches

### Navigating the Marketplace
1. Log in and click **Affiliate Marketplace** in the main menu.
2. Use the category filters or search bar to find products.

### Key Metrics to Watch
| Metric | What It Means | Ideal Range |
|--------|---------------|-------------|
| **Gravity** | Number of unique affiliates who earned a commission in the last 12 weeks | 20–100 (proven demand, manageable competition) |
| **Avg $/Sale** | Average earnings per sale | Varies by niche; higher is better |
| **Avg %/Sale** | Commission percentage per sale | 50%+ for digital products |
| **Recurring** | Whether the product pays ongoing commissions | Yes = passive income potential |

### Niche Search Terms to Use
- **Software/Tools**: "software", "SaaS", "productivity tools", "automation"
- **VPNs/Privacy**: "VPN", "privacy", "cybersecurity", "online security"
- **Web Hosting**: "website builder", "hosting", "WordPress", "domain"
- **Design Software**: "Photoshop", "design", "graphics", "video editing"

### Filtering Tips
- Sort by **Gravity** (descending) to see what is converting now.
- Filter by **Commission %** to prioritize high-payout offers.
- Look for products with **low refund rates** — check the product page or search for reviews.

## Commission Structure Overview

- **Standard digital products**: 50% – 75% per sale
- **Recurring/subscription products**: Ongoing commissions for the life of the customer
- **CPA (Cost Per Action) offers**: Flat payout per conversion (requires approval)
- **Physical products**: Lower commissions, typically 10% – 25%
- **Cookie duration**: 60 days (standard on most products)

> **Affiliate cost**: Free to join. Vendors pay activation fees; affiliates pay nothing.

## Payment Threshold and Methods

### Payment Threshold
- **Default**: $100
- **Minimum adjustable**: $10
- **Recommendation**: Keep at $100 to reduce processing overhead.

### Customer Distribution Requirement
To receive your **first payment**, ClickBank requires:
- At least **5 sales**
- Using **2 different payment methods** (e.g., Visa + PayPal)

This prevents self-referral abuse.

### Payout Schedule
- **Weekly** or **Bi-weekly** options available
- Payments are issued after the pay period closes

### Payment Methods
| Method | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| **Direct Deposit / ACH** | Fastest; available in supported countries |
| **Wire Transfer** | $25–$35 fee; 5–10 business days |
| **Paper Check** | Slowest; mailed to your address |
| **Payoneer** | Popular for international affiliates; provides a virtual US bank account |

### Tax Documentation
- US affiliates: Submit **W-9**
- International affiliates: Submit **W-8BEN** (or equivalent)

## Compliance Requirements

### FTC Disclosure Rules
- You **must** clearly disclose affiliate relationships on any page containing affiliate links.
- Disclosures must be **clear, conspicuous, and near the link** — not buried in a footer or separate page.
- Acceptable examples:
  - *"This post contains affiliate links. We earn a commission when you purchase through these links, at no extra cost to you."*
  - *"Disclosure: As a ClickBank affiliate, I earn from qualifying purchases."*

### ClickBank-Specific Rules
- **No misleading claims**: Do not promise guaranteed income, weight loss, or results you cannot substantiate.
- **No false scarcity**: Do not invent countdown timers or fake limited-time offers.
- **Approved landing pages**: If running paid ads, ensure the vendor allows it and the landing page is compliant.
- **No fraudulent traffic**: ClickBank actively monitors for bot traffic, incentivized clicks, and cookie stuffing.
- **Prohibited content**: Do not promote on adult sites, warez sites, or platforms violating ClickBank's acceptable use policy.

### Best Practices
- **Vet every product** before promoting. Buy it or research refund rates and reviews.
- **Keep disclosures visible** at the top of posts and near affiliate links.
- **Use tracking IDs** in your HopLinks to monitor which campaigns drive sales.
- **Stay updated** on ClickBank's policy changes via their [Support Center](https://support.clickbank.com).
