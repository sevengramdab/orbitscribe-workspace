# Etsy Listing Best Practices

## Title Format (140 chars max)
[Main Keyword] | [Style] | [Occasion] | [Gift For] | [Color/Size]
Example: "Celestial Wolf Art Print | Boho Wall Decor | Mystical Nursery | Gift for Her | 8x10 11x14"

## Description Template
1. Hook (1-2 sentences about the product benefit)
2. What's included (sizes, materials, packaging)
3. Perfect for (occasions, rooms, gift ideas)
4. Shipping info
5. Care instructions
6. Shop policies link

## Tags (13 tags, 20 chars each)
Use high-volume + long-tail mix:
- Wall art
- Digital download
- Boho decor
- Nursery print
- Celestial art
- Wolf poster
- Mystical gift
- Printable art
- Spiritual decor
- Bedroom wall art
- Gift for her
- Bohemian style
- Instant download

## Pricing Strategy
- Cost from Printify: ~$8-15
- Etsy fees: ~15% (listing + transaction + processing)
- Target margin: 50-70%
- Recommended price: $19.99 - $39.99
- Offer 3 sizes: Small ($19.99), Medium ($29.99), Large ($39.99)
