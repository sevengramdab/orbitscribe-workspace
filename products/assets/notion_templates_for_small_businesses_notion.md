# Notion Template: Notion Templates For Small Businesses

## Setup Guide

This template is designed for small businesses to streamline their operations and stay organized. Customize it according to your company's needs and use the built-in features of Notion to manage your team, projects, tasks, meetings, and documents.

## Template

# **Notion Templates for Small Businesses**

## Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Duplicate the Template
Duplicate this template to create a new page in your Notion workspace.

### Step 2: Customize Your Template
Rename the template and add your company's logo to the top of the page.
